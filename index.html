We empower youth developers and communities
through web development and technology.

Serving as interns at Synapps, teen developers can apply the skills they've learned
by creating websites, web apps, and mobile apps for local organizations for a reduced price.

Our Goal

We provide low-cost websites, web apps, and mobile apps for local organizations, especially nonprofits, by leveraging the skills of able high school developers.

Save Money

Tired of confusing, expensive options for creating a simple website for your organization? We offer reduced prices and offer step-by-step guidance.

Launch a Career

The Synapps Apprenticeship Program helps students gain work experience, network with industry professionals, and learn best practices in web development.

Community Service

The Apprenticeship Program reframes a students' web development careers within the context of helping others. Let's get more students changing the world through tech!
